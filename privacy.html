Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Vedanga Infratech's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website.

Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you - for example, your name, phone number, and message when you submit our contact form. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent, and let you know why we're collecting it and how it will be used.

How We Use It

The information we collect is used primarily to respond to your inquiries, provide quotes, and facilitate business communications regarding our scaffolding goods, steel tubes & pipes, and roofing sheet solutions. Submitting our contact form opens a WhatsApp conversation pre-filled with your details; that conversation is then handled directly between you and our team on WhatsApp.

Cookies & Third-Party Content

This website embeds a Google Maps view to help you locate our offices. Google may set its own cookies when this content loads, governed by Google's own privacy policy. We do not use tracking cookies of our own.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any information shared with us from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access, and we do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights

You are free to decline providing personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to respond to certain inquiries as a result. You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you or to request its removal.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
