How We Manufacture Scaffolding Goods & Accessories

Every stage of our scaffolding goods manufacturing is handled in-house at our Cuncolim facility, from raw tube preparation through to finished, painted product ready for dispatch.

1

Pipe Cutting

Raw tube stock is cut to length as the first step in preparing material for every scaffolding component we manufacture.

2

Three Roll Hydraulic Thread Rolling

Cut tube sections are threaded on our three-roll hydraulic thread rolling machines for a precise, consistent fit.

3

Ledger Welding

Ledgers are welded to spec, forming the horizontal members that tie scaffold frames together.

4

Prop Welding

Support props are welded and reinforced to carry structural loads on site.

5

Standard Cuplock Welding

Cuplock components are welded to our standard specification for fast, reliable on-site assembly.

6

Multi Spindle Prop Drilling

Finished props are drilled on our multi-spindle machines for accurate, repeatable hole placement.

7

Automatic Paint Conveyor

Every piece is finished on our automatic paint conveyor before being staged for dispatch.

All 7 processes above are carried out fully in-house at our Cuncolim facility.
