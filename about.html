About Us

Innovative Manufacturing

Our success is built on a foundation of quality, innovation, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Supported by modern manufacturing facilities, advanced production technology, and stringent quality control processes, we ensure every product meets the highest standards of performance, safety, and durability. Today, Vedanga Infratech operates 4 state-of-the-art manufacturing units based out of Goa, specializing in high-grade steel manufacturing, processing, and distribution. The company holds a total installed production capacity of 2,000 tons for roofing sheets and 5,000 tons for tubes & pipes, with a state-of-the-art facility for production of scaffolding goods.

Our Mission

To support global infrastructure growth through reliable manufacturing, efficient distribution, and long-term partnerships built on trust and quality, starting with India and expanding worldwide.

Our Vision

To become world's most trusted and preferred steel solutions provider by delivering quality products, innovative solutions, and exceptional customer service.

Our History

Company's Journey

2012

Established as a trading unit for color-coated roofing sheets in Verna, Goa.

2014

Set up our first roll forming unit for roofing sheets, built to match customised requirements.

2018

Scaled up to 2,000 tons/month capacity with a 2nd roll forming unit in Verna, Goa.

2021

Diversified into tubes & pipes, adding 5,000 tons/month capacity at Cuncolim IDC, Goa.

2026

Opened our 4th manufacturing unit for scaffolding goods & accessories at Cuncolim IDC, Goa.
